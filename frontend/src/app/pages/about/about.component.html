The service that our site offers is the ability to predict the value of the monthly rent that will bring the user the maximum profit, The prediction is made by checking the monthly rent of similar apartments and other parameters.

About company

The user will enter a number of parameters on the property he owns and can get a monthly prdiction return prediction. For the prediction, the system will collect Big Data and implement an algorithm to predict the monthly rent sum from the asset. To create a qualitative prediction fitted for environmental variables, we are currently focusing on one country. In addition, since we are talking about an economic decision and I order to give the user a sense of security, we will provide the user a summary of information about the property and its environment that influenced the expected return decision. For example, average similar property environment, graph with intersting data about similar properties, the environment suitable for this type of rental. The user experience on the site will be simple and easy, the amount of parameters required from the user is minimal and at the same time the information obtained is wide and creates a feeling that he "knows" the property, its environment and the demand for it and can make an informed decision according to his needs.
